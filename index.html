My Process

Take the stress out of building a website.

1. Discovery

  • Discuss your goals and determine success metrics.
  • Identify problems to solve and define visitors of the website.

2. UX Design

  • Design wireframes for the entire website to map out key customer journeys.
  • Understand what content will be needed to communicate your product/service.

3. Visual Design

  • Define visual direction of the website.
  • Translate wireframes into high-fidelity designs that communicate your brand’s product/service.

4. Launch

  • QA check on all web pages for accuracy.
  • Test every integration to ensure a seamless launch.
  • Celebrate your brand new website! 🎉
